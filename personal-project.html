Henry's Space Odyssey is a simple web app that makes use of the many API's available from the NASA API webpage

I designed this app for my nephew Henry, who has a passion for everything space related, and a dream to one day work for NASA or SpaceX

The goal of this project is to encourage curiosity in the world around us, and make learning about space, and NASA, fun.
